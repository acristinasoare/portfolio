A bit about myself

Originally from Transylvania, Romania, I moved to Scotland when I was 19 to study Dentistry at University of Dundee. I am currently working at Barron Dental, a private practice in Edinburgh.

After practicing dentistry for 7 years, I have decided to seek new challenges and my enthusiasm for problem-solving has led me to find out more about the possibility of learning programming skills. Guided by my own inquisitive research and the advice of an accomplished dental colleague who completed this course, I have decided to take a leap of faith and register with Command Shift. I am currently finding myself exploring the intricacies of web page development, brimming with excitement for the possibilities that lay ahead.

When not immersed in the world of dentistry or programming, I delight in exploring different regions of Scotland. I am particularly drawn to the stunning Scottish coastlines (Scotland has many pristine beaches) or hiking in the Highlands of Scotland.

As the weather in Scotland can be fairly unpredictable, I have learned to cerish the nature in all its moods, and enjoy an adventure come rain or shine.
